Introduction

Welcome to the world of true Wonders and adventure. A broken world where distrusted "Cursed" use, hide and try to control their superpowers while dealing with everyday struggles of being as powerful as they are unwanted. A world where the masters of their respective crafts are capable of defying probability to bring forth effects normally only seen in movies or fairy tales. A world where our advanced technology, along with the supposedly unbreakable laws of physics it was based on, has failed humanity - and yet also a world where genius (if slightly unstable) inventors are not only perfectly capable of building a working computer in their garage but could literally set it up with an AI so advanced that... it might actually fail the Turing test - on purpose, out of spite due to feeling hurt and insulted by having to take it in the first place.

Do you think that you could find your way around the new rules where the physics of action movies and superhero comics became the laws of formerly mundane reality? Would you want to try your hand at taking on the role of one of the exceptional inhabitants of this new world? If so, gather up your friends and buckle up for an adventure! It's the age of wonders - and you're invited!
